What are Light Sticks?

These are LED sticks powered by AAA batteries and include a Bluetooth capability. These things have a color changing feature which you can sync with other light sticks during a concert through Bluetooth. These light sticks are customized by each production company to represent an idol's group identity and the community behind them, called a fandom.
